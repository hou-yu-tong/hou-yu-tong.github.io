The first days in South China University of Technology (SCUT)

I joint an offline reserch about robots since I am interested in engineering area. This is the first time that I involve the design and application fields of robots. It is a liitle bit hard for me to write some codes or to give some specific ideas how to design a robot since I haven’t learnt anything about this before. I was distributed to do some work on searching the literature about our topic–Enabling Robots to Determine the Most Energy-Saving Path through Visual Sensors and Verbal Descriptions. There have already been a lot of extrodinary papers about the robotics, I had learnt something new during the searching process.
Unfortunately, I misunderstood the robot that our group would like to make. I thought that we need to make a camera,but actually we need to apply the camera on to the robot. I fixed the false just in a lunchbreak and I just had my heart in my mouth during the revision process as we needed to report it in lecture in the afternoon.
